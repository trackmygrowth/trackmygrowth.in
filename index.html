What is TrackMyGrowth?

TrackMyGrowth is an advanced student monitoring and school development platform designed to empower both parents and educational institutions. It provides real-time insights into a child’s academic progress, cognitive skills, social behavior, and overall well-being, ensuring a holistic approach to education.

For schools, TrackMyGrowth offers performance tracking, school inspections, faculty development, and ranking systems to help institutions improve teaching standards and infrastructure.

For parents, the platform delivers personalized reports, expert counseling, academic tracking, and behavioral analysis, making it easier to support a child’s growth and future success.

Tailored Reports

How TrackMyGrowth Creates Deeply Analyzed Reports?
At TrackMyGrowth, our report generation process is based on in-depth expert observation, structured evaluation, and data-driven insights to ensure a 360-degree assessment of every student.

🔹 Step 1: Expert-Led Video Interview
A panel of subject specialists, psychologists, and education counselors conducts a detailed one-on-one video call with the student to assess:
✅ Academic performance & subject understanding
✅ Cognitive skills & problem-solving ability
✅ Confidence, communication, and personality traits
✅ Social behavior & emotional intelligence
✅ Hygiene, health awareness & discipline

🔹 Step 2: Expert Panel Discussion & Observation
During the interview, our entire expert team takes notes, analyzes responses, and evaluates key aspects such as:
✔️ Learning speed & concept clarity
✔️ Speaking skills, body language & confidence level
✔️ Behavior in social and academic situations
✔️ Time management, study habits & focus ability

🔹 Step 3: Data Compilation & Detailed Evaluation
After the session, all observations are compiled and cross-examined by experts to ensure accuracy and depth in analysis.

🔹 Step 4: Personalized Report Generation
Based on the assessment, a customized report is created, including:
📌 Subject-wise strengths & improvement areas
📌 Study strategies & focused learning plans
📌 Confidence & communication enhancement tips
📌 Career guidance based on aptitude & interest
📌 Health, hygiene & personality development recommendations

🔹 Step 5: Report Delivery & Parental Guidance
The final detailed report is shared with students and parents via email & WhatsApp, with an optional counseling session to help them implement expert recommendations.

🚀 This deep observational approach ensures that every student receives a truly insightful, data-backed, and actionable growth plan for academic and personal success!
