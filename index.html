What is TrackMyGrowth?

TrackMyGrowth is an advanced student monitoring and school development platform designed to empower both parents and educational institutions. It provides real-time insights into a child’s academic progress, cognitive skills, social behavior, and overall well-being, ensuring a holistic approach to education.

For schools, TrackMyGrowth offers performance tracking, school inspections, faculty development, and ranking systems to help institutions improve teaching standards and infrastructure.

For parents, the platform delivers personalized reports, expert counseling, academic tracking, and behavioral analysis, making it easier to support a child’s growth and future success.

🚀 With TrackMyGrowth, education becomes data-driven, insightful, and transformative—ensuring that every student reaches their full potential!

How does it help schools?

TrackMyGrowth empowers schools by providing data-driven insights, performance monitoring, and expert support to enhance educational quality and student success.

✅ Key Benefits for Schools:

🔹 Academic Performance Monitoring – Regular assessments, student progress tracking, and city-wise benchmarking to improve learning outcomes.

🔹 School Inspections & Quality Audits – Monthly evaluations of infrastructure, hygiene, water/air quality, classroom conditions, and transport safety to ensure a safe learning environment.

🔹 Faculty Development & Teacher Support – Expert-led teacher training programs, modern teaching methods, and assistance in hiring qualified faculty for better education delivery.

🔹 Growth & Ranking System – Confidential performance reports comparing schools city-wide, helping institutions identify improvement areas and attract more students.

🔹 Exam Support & Extra Classes – Special exam preparation sessions, study plans, and expert-led classes to boost student performance.

🚀 TrackMyGrowth helps schools set new benchmarks for excellence, ensuring holistic development for students while improving institutional reputation and academic success!
